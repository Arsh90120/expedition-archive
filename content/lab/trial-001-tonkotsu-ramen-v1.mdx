## TRIAL PARAMETERS

First attempt at homemade tonkotsu from scratch. Target: full broth from pork bones, tare, soft egg, chashu.

## EXECUTION LOG

- Broth simmered 2 hours (insufficient)
- Added chili oil — too much, significantly too much
- Soft egg overcooked, texture off
- Chashu was the only success

## INCIDENT REPORT

Broth was thin. Spice level violated multiple international agreements. Could not finish the bowl.

## VERDICT

DISCONTINUED pending full recipe revision. v2 will triple broth time and treat chili oil as a controlled substance.
