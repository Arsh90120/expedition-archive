## BRIEFING

First time above treeline. I picked Tuckerman Ravine, forecast said mild, wind had other plans once I got to elevation.

## EQUIPMENT MANIFEST

- Osprey Atmos 65 AG
- Merino wool base + mid layer
- Salomon X Ultra 4 GTX
- Jetboil Flash + instant ramen (field ration)
- Black Diamond Spot headlamp
- Emergency bivvy

## DEBRIEF

Summit ridge at 1142. Wind chill sitting at -3°C by the time I got there. The views were genuinely worth it. 4,200ft of gain over 8.4 miles total.

## INCIDENT REPORT

- Underestimated wind above treeline — cotton layer was a mistake
- Started 20 minutes late, lost light on descent
- Jetboil struggled to ignite in wind above 3,500ft
- Would add wind barrier for stove next time

## VERDICT

Mission successful. Clearance Level 4 earned.
