## BRIEFING

First real expedition above treeline. Target: summit ridge via Tuckerman Ravine trail. Conditions forecasted mild but wind shifted hard at elevation.

## EQUIPMENT MANIFEST

- Osprey Atmos 65 AG
- Merino wool base + mid layer
- Salomon X Ultra 4 GTX
- Jetboil Flash + instant ramen (field ration)
- Black Diamond Spot headlamp
- Emergency bivvy

## DEBRIEF

Reached summit ridge at 1142. Wind chill dropped to -3°C. Views were worth every meter of elevation gain. Total ascent 4,200ft over 8.4 miles.

## INCIDENT REPORT

- Underestimated wind above treeline — cotton layer was a mistake
- Started 20 minutes late, lost light on descent
- Jetboil struggled to ignite in wind above 3,500ft
- Would add wind barrier for stove next time

## VERDICT

Mission successful. Clearance Level 4 earned.
